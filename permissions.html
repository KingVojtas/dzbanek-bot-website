Permissions explained

dzbanek-bot asks only for what it needs — not Administrator. Here’s exactly why each permission appears on the invite screen.

Permission Used for Why it matters
View Channel Music, news, deals Required to see text/voice channels where the bot posts or joins.
Send Messages Replies & digests Slash command replies, now-playing embeds, RSS posts, deal digests.
Embed Links Rich embeds Discord embeds for tracks, news articles, Steam/Epic deals.
Manage Messages Channel cleanup Deletes the bot’s own previous digest message before posting a new Steam or Epic update so channels stay tidy — not for moderating your members.
Connect Music Join the voice channel you are in for /play.
Speak Music Play audio in voice channels.
